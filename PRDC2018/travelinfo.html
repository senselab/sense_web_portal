Transportation Information

PRDC 2018 will be held in Academia Sinica, Nangang District, Taipei, Taiwan. You can reach Taiwan by booking a flight that is bound for Taiwan Taoyuan International Airport, which is approximately 60 kilometers in distance to Academia Sinica. You may also look for flights that are bound for Taipei Songshan Airport, which is approximately 10 kilometers in distance to Academia Sinica. Taipei Songshan Airport is a regional airport. Taipei Songshan Airport has direct flights from/to Haneda airport (Japan) and Jeju airport (South Korea)

Local Taxi

Taxis are available outside the arrival halls of both Terminal 1 and Terminal 2 of Taoyuan International Airport

The charge is conventionally based on the meter plus a 50% surcharge. On average, a trip from Taoyuan International Airport to Academia Sinica by taxi costs about NT$1,700.

Service Hours

24 hours a day.

 By Taipei Metro

Taoyuan Airport MRT

MRT is available from either Terminal 1 and Terminal 2 of Taoyuan International Airport

Travel Details and Fare

From Terminal 1 or from Terminal 2 you can board in to the Train (Express/ Commuter) towards Taipei Main Station. The ticket counters and the ticket issuing machines are available in front of the entrance of metro station in the airport. This costs around $160. The fare for taking Express train and Commuter Train is same. Express Trains are purple color and Commuter Trains are Blue.

From Taipei Main Station, transfer to the Taipei MRT Blue Line (Bannan Line or BL). Get down ta Nangang MRT station. A Taxi ride from Nangang MRT station to Academia Sinica cost about NT$100 – NT$110 or you may take the local bus service (306/ 620/ 645) which was mentioned above.

Service Hours

06:00 AM to 11:30 PM

Time of Travel

Approximately 35 minutes (Express) or 50 minutes (Commuter) from Terminal 1 or Terminal 2 to Taipei Main Station.

Recommendation
